Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Here, we have collected the most common questions about IT Development and the answers to them. If you can’t find what you’re looking for then feel free to ask us directly via contact form.
How is Staff Augmentation different from outsourcing?

With augmentation, you’re gaining individual contributors who work as part of your team. In outsourcing, you’re delegating an entire project or function to an external company.

What is the concept of Product Development?

Taking a product idea from concept to launch, involving research, design, development, and testing. It all comes in the umbrella of product development.

What's the newest trend in the IT world?

AI, automation, cloud, cybersecurity, and the Internet of Things (IoT) are reshaping the world of IT.

How often one should post on Social Media?

Depends on the platform, but consistency is key. Aim for at least a few times a week.

What is the main difference between Ui and Ux?

UI (User Interface) is the look & feel; UX (User Experience) is how users interact with the product.
